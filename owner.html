Meet the Owner

The Doughty Delights was born out of a love for exceptional coffee and a desire to create a cozy space where friends gather, ideas flow, and the world slows down for a moment. Our journey begins with sourcing the finest beans, roasting them with precision, and blending flavors that dance on your palate.


Our cafe, a culinary venture born from the collective passion and friendship of five dedicated partners. Our journey began with a shared love for exceptional food, artful coffee, and creating memorable experiences. Each partner brings a distinct set of skills and expertise, contributing to the diverse tapestry that makes our cafe truly special


Join us at The Doughty Delights, where every visit is an invitation to savor the collaborative spirit that defines our cafe—a place where coffee, dessert, and pasta come together in perfect harmony, creating an artful experience for every palate.
